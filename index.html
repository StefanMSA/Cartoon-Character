Byakuya Kuchiki

About Byakuya

Byakuya Kuchiki (朽木 白哉, Kuchiki Byakuya) is the 28th head of the Kuchiki Clan, one of the four great noble clans in Soul Society. He is also the captain of the 6th Division in the Gotei 13.

Byakuya and some of the other soul reapers.

  2. Shunpo Master:

  3. Kidō Master
  4. Hakuda Combatant
  5. Immense Spiritual Power
  6. Master Tactician
